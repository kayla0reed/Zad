Welcome to ZÄD Modern Mediterranean

About

Equal parts restaurant, cafe and marketplace, ZÄD embraces the pure, simple flavors from the many countries that comprise the eastern Mediterranean region with a vibrant modern twist.

Our goal is to use the finest ingredients available in order to present Mediterranean cuisine at it's best. Dine in or carry out, our passion for food, personalized service and a modern aesthetic combines with centuries old tradition to bring you a taste you can savor.

From the arc of the Levant, from Athens to Marrakech, Istanbul to Jerusalem, Eastern Mediterranean cuisine is synonymous with a healthy diet and where possible, all our ingredients are locally produced, seasonal and organic.

ZÄD is a place where old world leisure comfortably abides with new world convenience, so whether you drop in for a carry out or settle down for a feast you’ll be part of the bustle of a classic Mediterranean café.
